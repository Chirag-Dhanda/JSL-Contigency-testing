ABOUT JSL

India's Leading Stainless Steel Manufacturer

Who We Are

Jindal Stainless Limited (JSL) is one of India's leading stainless steel manufacturers, serving automotive, railway, infrastructure, architecture, food processing, consumer goods, engineering and energy sectors.

JSL focuses on quality, innovation, sustainability and operational excellence, supported by modern manufacturing plants and digital technologies including SAP.
